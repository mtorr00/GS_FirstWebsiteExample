Google as a developer resource:

Google has a variety of features beyond it's search engine that help aid developers in the creation process.

Google API's are accessible for very little for bedroom programmers, such that easy exploration of concepts can be achieved for free.

Google also has a database of fonts that front end developers can freely use to aid their website design.
